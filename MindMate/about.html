Tomorrow

Tomorrow allows you to inspire your future self using the power of your own voice. Record yourself today, get it tomorrow, and then, it's gone the following day. Tomorrow gives you a chance to provide your future self with any goals, affirmations, or mental notes that you think of today, tomorrow. Tomorrow hopes to bring you a simple way to make your goals more attainable and help you remember all of the fleeting moments of inspiration that you have throughout your day.
